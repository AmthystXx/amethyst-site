Geode

About Geode

Geode is a datapack library for the newest version of Minecraft. When new versions of the game comes out, changes to Geode will likely not be made retroactively.
Geode adds the ability to add custom blocks, items, and totems; add right click detection to your items; and save arbitrary data to players or other entities.
Future features include:
Custom arrows and raycasting for items.
